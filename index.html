§ 02 — FAQ

Frequently Asked常见问题

What platforms does Quickmark run on?

Quickmark is a native Mac application and requires macOS 11 (Big Sur) or later. It runs natively on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.

Quickmark 是一款 Mac 原生应用,系统要求为 macOS 11 (Big Sur) 或更高版本。在 Apple Silicon 和 Intel 芯片上都可原生运行。

Where are my documents saved?

Your documents are stored wherever you save them — Quickmark never moves, copies, or uploads your files. Pasted or dragged-in images are stored locally inside the app's sandbox container.

你的文档保存在你自己选择的位置,Quickmark 不会移动、复制或上传你的文件。粘贴或拖入的图片会保存在应用沙盒容器内的本地目录。

Does Quickmark work offline?

Yes. All Markdown rendering, syntax highlighting, math typesetting (LaTeX via MathJax), and diagram rendering (Mermaid) run entirely on your device. No internet connection is required, and nothing is ever sent to our servers.

是的。Markdown 渲染、代码高亮、LaTeX 数学公式、Mermaid 图表全部在本地运行,无需联网,没有任何数据上传到我们的服务器。

How do I export to PDF or HTML?

Use File → Export from the menu bar, then choose PDF or HTML. Adjust page margins in Preferences if needed.

通过菜单栏的 文件 → 导出 选择 PDF 或 HTML 格式。如需调整页边距,请前往 偏好设置。

How do I completely remove the app and its data?

Drag Quickmark from your Applications folder to the Trash. To remove local preferences and cached assets as well, delete the folder at ~/Library/Containers/com.quickmark.app/.

把 Quickmark 从"应用程序"文件夹拖到废纸篓即可。如需同时删除偏好设置和本地缓存,请删除 ~/Library/Containers/com.quickmark.app/ 目录。

§ 03 — Privacy

Privacy PolicyEnglish

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Quickmark ("we", "our", or "the app") is a native Mac Markdown editor. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what happens to your data when you use the app.

1. Data we collect

None. Quickmark does not collect, transmit, store on our servers, or share any personal information, usage statistics, analytics, crash reports, or user-generated content of any kind. There is no account system. You do not need to sign in to use the app.

2. Data stored locally on your device

The following data stays on your Mac and never leaves it:

  • Markdown documents you create or open
  • Images you drag, paste, or import (saved under ~/Library/Containers/com.quickmark.app/Data/Library/Application Support/Quickmark/Assets/)
  • Recent files list and security-scoped bookmarks
  • User preferences (theme, font size, export padding, etc.) stored in the app's sandboxed UserDefaults

You can delete all local data at any time by removing the app from your Applications folder and clearing the container directory at ~/Library/Containers/com.quickmark.app/.

3. Network usage

Quickmark does not make any outgoing network requests on its own. The preview pane renders Markdown in an embedded WKWebView. If your document contains external image links (e.g. ![](https://...)), the system will fetch those images for display. No other network activity occurs.

4. Third-party components

The preview is powered by the following open-source JavaScript libraries, all bundled inside the app — nothing is loaded from a CDN:

  • marked (MIT License)
  • highlight.js (BSD 3-Clause License)
  • MathJax (Apache License 2.0)
  • Mermaid (MIT License)

5. Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

6. Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact: weta_zheng@qq.com

§ 04 — 隐私

隐私政策中文

最后更新:2026 年 4 月 22 日

Quickmark(下称"本应用")是一款 Mac 原生 Markdown 编辑器。我们重视并尊重你的隐私。本政策说明使用本应用时你的数据如何被处理。

1. 我们收集的数据

没有。本应用不收集、不上传、不保存到我们的服务器、不与任何第三方共享任何个人信息、使用行为、统计数据、崩溃日志或用户创作内容。本应用不要求注册账号,也无需登录。

2. 保存在你设备上的数据

以下数据全部保存在你的 Mac 本地,不会离开你的设备:

  • 你在应用中编辑或打开的 Markdown 文档
  • 你通过拖拽、粘贴、导入的图片(保存于 ~/Library/Containers/com.quickmark.app/Data/Library/Application Support/Quickmark/Assets/)
  • 最近文件列表与安全作用域书签
  • 偏好设置(主题、字号、导出边距等),保存于沙盒内的 UserDefaults

你随时可以从"应用程序"里删除本应用,并清理 ~/Library/Containers/com.quickmark.app/ 目录下的容器文件夹,以彻底删除所有本地数据。

3. 网络访问

本应用自身不主动发起任何网络请求。预览区域通过内置的 WKWebView 渲染 Markdown。如果你的文档里写了外链图片(例如 ![](https://...)),系统会去拉取这些图片以显示;除此以外没有任何网络通信。

4. 第三方组件

预览区由以下开源 JavaScript 库驱动,全部离线内置在 App 包内,不从 CDN 加载:

  • marked(MIT 协议)
  • highlight.js(BSD 3-Clause 协议)
  • MathJax(Apache 2.0 协议)
  • Mermaid(MIT 协议)

5. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童,也不会主动收集任何人的个人信息。

6. 政策变更

若本政策有更新,我们会在同一 URL 发布新版本并更新上方"最后更新"日期。

7. 联系方式
